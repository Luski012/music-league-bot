TrackBattle Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Information Collected

TrackBattle stores:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Guild IDs
  • Song submission data
  • Vote data
  • Win/loss statistics

Information Not Collected

TrackBattle does NOT collect:

  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • Direct messages
  • Message content
  • Payment information

Purpose of Data

Data is used solely to operate music competitions and maintain leaderboards.

Data Sharing

TrackBattle does not sell or share user data with third parties.

Data Removal

Server administrators may remove TrackBattle at any time.
